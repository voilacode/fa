Frequently Asked Questions

Can you create an English test at different levels? + −

Yes we can. We specialise in creating tests that assess a candidate's proficiency in English. We also create tests that are tailored to filter candidate of a particular proficiency level to ensure you get the perfect fit.

We want our workforce to be familiar with words in a particular domain, can you do that? + −

Yes! Depending on the the industry, we can tailor courses that will make your workforce proficient in the most commonly used vocabulary in that domain. From healthcare to law, we have created courses for different industries.

Do you have a platform only for tests? + −

Absolutely. If you just want a battery of tests, we could plan a series of tests or you could use your own questions to create tests on our platform. We could whitelabel our platform for you!

Can we have a course of a particular duration? + −

Before we start of creating a custom course, we take into account all possible variables. From budgeting time to budgeting costs, everything can be tailored to your requirements.
