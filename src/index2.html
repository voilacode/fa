ACT Preparation Steps

Our program includes a series of steps designed to fully prepare you for the ACT exam.

Follow each step for a comprehensive and guided preparation experience. Select a step to view details.

Diagnostic Test & Evaluation

The diagnostic test will help in gauging the student's level of test-readiness. This will help in formulating a plan that works best for the individual.

Comprehensive Explanations and Strategy

Our course is designed to be completed within 150 to 180 hours. This timeline encompasses 10 to 12 weeks dedicated to completion of the syllabus.

In-course Assessments

From the second week of the program, we recommend all students to make full use of our custom practice portal to begin their exposure to both authentic and exam-grade questions to help identify problem areas and to familiarize them with the question format.

Full-Length Tests

With the strategy and practice done, the final step toward the exam is dedicated to comprehensive revision, ensuring our students are fully prepared and confident.

Second Attempt Support

In the unlikely event that a student does not achieve their target score, we provide comprehensive sectional tests and booster sessions to review essential topics.
